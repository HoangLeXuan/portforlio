AI/ML Timeline Project

A comprehensive timeline tracking key milestones in artificial intelligence and machine learning

What is it?

An interactive and visually structured timeline that illustrates key milestones in artificial intelligence and machine learning from the 1950s to the present day. Built using Lucidchart and embedded in a web interface, the timeline offers users an intuitive way to explore the evolution of AI across major historical events, technological breakthroughs, and paradigm shifts.

Objective

The goal of this project was to learn about Lucidchart and apply research and communication skills to create an educational resource that maps out the historical development of AI. I wanted to present complex information in a digestible, engaging format that would be helpful for students, professionals, and recruiters alike—particularly those seeking a high-level overview of how AI has progressed over time.

Tools

Development:

  • Lucidchart

Value Proposition

This timeline showcases my ability to synthesize technical research into a clear and engaging visualization. It reflects my strengths in historical analysis, information design, and audience-centric presentation. Whether used in a classroom, portfolio, or professional presentation, this artifact demonstrates both domain knowledge in AI and the technical capability to build a polished, user-friendly learning tool.

What I Learned

Originally designed for presentation use, I later revised the structure using Lucidchart to suit viewers.
